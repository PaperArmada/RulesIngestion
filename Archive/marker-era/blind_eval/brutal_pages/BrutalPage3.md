## BARACHIUS HELM
Barachius angels serve as divine vanquishers to benevolent gods—purging technology across the Universe deemed to be twisted or evil. Their philosophies on what constitutes evil can be unclear, as these angels might hunt down an individual malfunctioning robot yet leave nuclear missile silos untouched.

The magnificent helmet worn by a barachius angel is an extension of their body and can't be taken by force without utterly killing the angel. Those few mortal champions who earn the barachius's respect are sometimes gifted a digital copy of the angel's helm, which are rumored to possess some of their celestial power.
